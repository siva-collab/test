Mud Pot Do's

  • Season your clay pot before first use by soaking it in water for several hours or overnight.
  • Gradually increase the heat when cooking with a clay pot to prevent cracking.
  • Preheat the clay pot slowly to avoid thermal shock.
  • Use low to medium heat settings when cooking with clay pots.
  • Soak the clay pot in water for at least 15-20 minutes before each use to help retain moisture during cooking.
  • Use wooden or silicone utensils to avoid scratching the surface of the clay pot.
  • Clean your clay pot with warm water and a soft sponge or cloth. Avoid using abrasive cleaners or scrubbers.
  • Store your clay pot in a cool, dry place to prevent mold growth.
