Mud Pot Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice, soaked for 30 minutes
  • Assorted vegetables (like carrots, beans, peas, cauliflower), chopped
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh mint and coriander leaves for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons ghee or oil

Procedure:

  • 1.Heat ghee or oil in a mud pot over medium heat.
  • 2.Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  • 3.Add sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  • 4.Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, sauté for 2 minutes.
  • 5.Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • 6.Add garam masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt.
  • 7.Mix well.
  • 8.Add chopped vegetables and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  • 9.Drain the soaked rice and add it to the pot. Mix gently with the vegetables.
  • 10.Add 4 cups of water and bring it to a boil.
  • 11.Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the mud pot, and let the biryani cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  • 12.Garnish with fresh mint and coriander leaves before serving. Serve hot with raita or yogurt.
