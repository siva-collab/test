Mud Pot Chicken Curry

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken pieces
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Procedure:

  • 1.Heat oil in a mud pot over medium heat.
  • 2.Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  • 3.Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, sauté for 2 minutes.
  • 4.Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
  • 4.Add coriander powder, cumin powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well.
  • 5.Add chicken pieces and coat them well with the masala.
  • 7.Cover the mud pot and let the chicken cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes or until tender.
  • 8.Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with rice or roti.
